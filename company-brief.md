The Vision

Kiraa exists to put the power of enterprise-grade AI directly into the hands of the people who run businesses every day — the analysts, operators, planners and finance teams whose judgement keeps companies moving. We believe the future of work is not one in which AI quietly removes the human from the decision. It is one in which AI lets every employee see further, think more clearly, and act faster than ever before.

The current narrative around AI is one of substitution: cut costs, cut headcount, cut institutional knowledge. We reject that framing. The most valuable asset in any business is the accumulated expertise of its people, and the goal of technology should be to amplify that expertise, not to discard it. AI that eliminates employees also eliminates the very judgement, context and craft that make a business defensible. AI that augments them compounds it.

On-Premise by Design: Safe, Fast, Secure
Kiraa’s patent-pending Cache Augmented Generation (CAG) architecture runs entirely on customer premises. Data never leaves the building. There are no token meters, no third-party cloud dependencies, no foreign jurisdictions sitting between a business and its own information. For regulated industries, mid-market manufacturers, logistics operators and retailers, this is not a feature — it is a precondition for using AI at all.

Safe. Sovereign by default. Customer data, customer prompts, customer answers — all stay on the customer’s hardware. No exfiltration risk. No legal-privilege risk. No quiet training of someone else’s model on your trade secrets.
Fast. CAG eliminates the retrieval and re-embedding overhead of conventional RAG pipelines. Answers arrive in moments, not minutes. Month-end reporting that used to take ten days runs in under an hour.
Secure. Hardware-backed integrity, no exposed API surface to the open internet, and a deployment model that satisfies the most demanding compliance, audit and data-sovereignty requirements.
Leveraging Apple to Democratise Analytics
Enterprise AI has, until now, been the privilege of organisations large enough to fund GPU clusters, cloud commitments and specialist data-science teams. Kiraa changes the economics. By building on Apple Silicon — the Mac Studio, with its unified memory, Metal GPU acceleration and Memory Integrity Enforcement — we deliver enterprise-grade inference on hardware that fits under a desk, costs a fraction of a server rack, and consumes a fraction of the power.

This is what democratising analytics actually looks like. A mid-market manufacturer in regional Australia, a logistics operator with twelve sites, a retailer with a single warehouse — each can now run the same calibre of AI that previously sat behind seven-figure cloud contracts. No token bills. No vendor lock-in. No need to send sensitive operational data to another continent. The competitive moat that hyperscale infrastructure once created is being collapsed by silicon that anyone can buy off the shelf.

The Mission
To empower every employee to make better decisions, faster.

That is the whole of it. Better, because the answers are grounded in the organisation’s own data, its own history and its own context. Faster, because waiting a week for a report from a central analytics team is no longer the rate-limiting step. Every employee, because analytics should not be a privilege reserved for those who can write SQL or wait in the BI queue.

When an operator on the factory floor can ask a question of the data and get an answer in seconds, the entire organisation moves faster. When a finance manager can interrogate a variance without raising a ticket, decisions get made the same day they need to be made. That is the compounding return on augmenting people: it doesn’t just speed up one role, it speeds up the whole company.

Our Values
Five values guide how we build, how we sell and how we treat one another.

Curiosity
We ask why before we ask how. Curiosity is what led us to question the assumption that AI must live in someone else’s cloud, and what keeps us looking for better architectures, better hardware, better ways to turn data into decisions. We hire for it and we reward it.

Integrity
We tell customers what our technology can do and, just as importantly, what it cannot. We don’t market hallucinations as features. We don’t hide costs in token meters. We protect customer data because we said we would, and because it is the right thing to do.

Grit
Building a sovereign AI platform from the Gold Coast, against the largest technology companies in the world, requires a particular kind of stubbornness. We show up, we ship, and we keep going when the easier path would be to fold into someone else’s stack.

Inclusion
Talent is not distributed by postcode, university or career stage. Our team includes a software-engineering university dropout building production systems, a working actuary contributing to open-source numerical libraries, and operators from heavy industry. We make room for capability wherever we find it.

Customer
The customer is not a logo on a slide. The customer is a CFO who needs a clean answer before a board meeting, a plant manager who needs to know why yield dropped overnight, a finance team that needs to close the month without working the weekend. Every architectural choice, every product decision and every commercial term is judged against whether it serves them.
